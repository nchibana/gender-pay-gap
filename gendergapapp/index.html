THE GENDER WAGE GAP

The Impact of Marital Status

and Parenthood

We analyzed the pay gap across 467 U.S. occupations. According to our analysis, the main source of the pay gap lies in the difference between the number of hours spent at work by women and men. On average, women work less hours after marriage and parenthood, while men work more.
